## 🎯 Unique Innovations

### Innovation #1: Payment Channel Architecture
```
Traditional (x402/MPP Charge):
  100 AI steps = 100 on-chain transactions = $1.00 in fees

Pulsar (MPP Session):
  100 AI steps = 100 off-chain commitments + 1 settlement = $0.01 in fees
  
Savings: 99% cost reduction
```

### Innovation #2: Agent Marketplace
First implementation of multi-tier agent pricing on payment channels:
- Research Agent: $0.05/step
- Coding Agent: $0.08/step
- Data Analyst: $0.06/step
- General Agent: $0.04/step

### Innovation #3: Partial Settlement
Keep channel open while paying incrementally:
- Long-running tasks don't need full settlement
- Subscription-like model possible
- Better capital efficiency

### Innovation #4: Real-Time Cost Tracking
Live SSE updates showing:
- Current step execution
- Cumulative cost
- Remaining budget
- Budget exhaustion prevention
